Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Toil, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Friendly Challenge mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Who We Are

Friendly Challenge is operated by Toil, LLC, a limited liability company registered in the State of Utah, United States.

For any privacy-related questions or requests, you can contact us at: friendlychallengeteam@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name you choose. Your email is used to authenticate your account.
  • Activity and challenge data: When you use the App, we collect the information you enter, including the challenges you create or join, the activities you log (such as activity type, value, and date), and your progress toward challenge goals.
  • Messages: If you post messages on a challenge's encouragement board, we collect and store those messages along with your display name.

Information collected automatically

  • Device push token: If you enable push notifications, we collect a device-specific token that allows us to send notifications to your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Basic technical information: Standard information necessary for the App to function, handled through our service providers described below.

We do not collect your precise location, we do not use third-party advertising or analytics tracking in the current version of the App, and we do not collect payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide the App's core features, including creating and joining challenges, logging activity, displaying leaderboards, and connecting you with friends
  • Send you notifications related to your challenges and account (if you enable them)
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the App
  • Respond to your questions and support requests

Information Visible to Other Users

Friendly Challenge is a social app, so some of your information is visible to other people who use it. Please be aware that:

  • Your display name and progress are visible to other participants in any challenge you join, including on leaderboards.
  • Messages you post on a challenge's encouragement board are visible to all participants in that challenge.
  • Your public profile (display name, and limited summary information such as challenge and friend counts) may be visible to other users who search for you or with whom you connect.
  • Friend requests and challenge invitations reveal your identity to the people you send them to.

Please only share information you are comfortable making visible to other participants.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase (provided by Google LLC) to operate the App, including authentication, database storage (Cloud Firestore), and push notification delivery. Your information is stored on and processed through Google's infrastructure on our behalf. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's own privacy terms.
  • Other users: As described in the section above, certain information is shared with other participants as part of the App's social features.
  • Legal reasons: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Toil, LLC, our users, or others.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active.

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us at friendlychallengeteam@gmail.com. Upon request, we will delete your account and personal data, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal purposes.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including relying on the security infrastructure provided by Google Firebase. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this Policy. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.
